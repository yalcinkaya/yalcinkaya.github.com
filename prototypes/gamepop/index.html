Pre-Order FAQs

How much is my card being charged?

When you pre-order today, nothing. When the GamePop console and controller ships to you, you will be charged 6.99 for the first month, plus 9.95 shipping (US) or 19.90 shipping (International). Then you'll be charged 6.99 each month.

Is there a contract?

While there is no contract per se, if you decide to terminate your subscription within the first 12 months you must send the console and controller back to BlueStacks in working order within 30 days of canceling. There is a $25 restocking fee. If you do not send the unit back there is a $100 early termination charge.

What do I get with the subscription?

GamePop will bring you access to the top games and apps on mobile that you already love like Talking Tom 2, Jetpack Joyride, Kids Shape Puzzle and 500+ more, constantly updated. These include over $200 in paid mobile games! GamePop pays for itself the moment it arrives at your house.

When will I receive my console?

GamePop is currently in development with an anticipated winter release.

What is the hardware inside the system and what does the controller look like?

We have not yet announced this level of detail but suffice it to say all games will run as fast or faster than they do on a top mobile phone. The controller is like none seen before and we'll be sharing more details soon.

Will I always be able to get the console and controller free?

No! Together they are a $100 value and are free for a limited time only. Pre-order while you can!
